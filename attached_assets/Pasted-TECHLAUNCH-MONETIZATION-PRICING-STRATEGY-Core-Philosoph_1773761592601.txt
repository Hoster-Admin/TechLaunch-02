TECHLAUNCH – MONETIZATION & PRICING STRATEGY				
Core Philosophy: Sign up, post a product, and engage with the community is always FREE				
				
🟢 WHAT IS ALWAYS FREE				
Feature	Why It Must Stay Free			
Create an account / Sign up	Growth depends on low barrier to entry. Every user is a potential community member or buyer.			
Post a product	More products = more content = more traffic. Restricting this kills the platform early.			
Upvote / Bookmark a product	Core engagement mechanic. Restricting it kills discovery.			
Comment on products	Community credibility depends on open discussion.			
Browse all products and categories	Discovery must be free or you lose users before they convert.			
Follow other users	Social graph building — essential for retention.			
Receive messages	Blocking incoming messages frustrates free users and reduces deal flow.
