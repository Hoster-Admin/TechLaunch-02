Tech Launch MENA — Public Site UI/UX Audit
You are a senior UX designer auditing the public site of Tech Launch (tlmena.com) — a Product Hunt + LinkedIn + AngelList hybrid for the MENA/Arab world.

CONTEXT

Market: MENA/GCC, bilingual Arabic + English
Users: Founders, Investors, Startups, Accelerators
Stack: React, DM Sans + DM Mono fonts, Noto Kufi Arabic for Arabic
Nav: Home, Join Launcher, List ▼, Search, Sign In, Join Now
Key pages: Homepage, Directory, Launcher (community), Companies, Suggestions


AUDIT THESE PAGES:
1. Homepage

Is it immediately clear what this platform is for?
Is the value proposition visible without scrolling?
Is the CTA ("Join Now" vs "Join Launcher") clear — do they mean different things?
Is the product feed compelling on first visit with zero products?
Is the Featured / Spotlight section visible and impactful?

2. Navigation

Is "Join Launcher" vs "Join Now" confusing to a new visitor?
Is the List dropdown organized logically?
Is search prominent enough?
On mobile — does the nav collapse correctly?

3. Directory Page

Does showing 20 industries all at 0 products hurt first impressions?
Is browse by Industry vs Country intuitive?
What should the empty state look like to still feel valuable?

4. Launcher / Community Page

Is the purpose of this page clear on arrival?
Are Articles / Posts / People tabs the right organization?
Is there a clear CTA to contribute — or does it feel read-only?
Do the article cards give enough info to decide whether to click?

5. Companies / List Pages

Does the empty state with "Nothing here yet" damage trust?
Is "+ List Your Company" the right primary CTA on an empty page?
Are the Country and Industry filters useful when there's no content?

6. Suggestions Page

Is "Sign in to send a suggestion" the right gate — or does it feel like a wall?
Should anonymous suggestions be allowed?
Is the page copy compelling enough to make users want to contribute?

7. Footer

Are there links that will 404 (Weekly Digest, Events, Write for Us, About, Contact)?
Does the footer give a good impression of platform completeness?
Should unbuilt pages be hidden or show a "Coming Soon" state?

8. Empty States Across All Pages

Do empty states feel like a broken product or an invite to be first?
Is there a consistent empty state design language?
Do empty states have a clear CTA pointing the user somewhere useful?

9. Onboarding & Conversion

Is it obvious what a new user should do first?
Is the signup flow frictionless?
After signup — does the user land somewhere useful or feel lost?
Is there a clear path for a founder vs an investor vs an accelerator?

10. Mobile & Responsiveness

Does the homepage feel good on mobile?
Are cards and buttons touch-friendly?
Does the directory grid collapse correctly on small screens?

11. Trust & Credibility

Does the platform look credible enough for a MENA founder to submit their product?
Are there social proof elements — user count, product count, community size?
Does the "Made with ❤️ in MENA" footer feel authentic or generic?

12. RTL & Arabic Readiness

Is there any RTL support currently?
Are Arabic strings handled correctly in cards and truncation?
Is Noto Kufi Arabic loaded and applied?


OUTPUT FORMAT
For each page:

✅ What works well
🔴 Critical issues (broken, confusing, or damaging to trust)
🟡 Improvements (friction or missing polish)
Specific fix recommendation for each issue

End with:

Overall UX score out of 100
Top 5 highest impact fixes before launch
Verdict: READY FOR LAUNCH or NEEDS WORK BEFORE LAUNCH
